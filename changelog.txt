!!! --- Q3Rally Changelog --- !!!

new in v0.8

Fixed:

- Race distance counter now works correctly on maps with single-brush checkpoint entities
  (e.g. q3r_valley). The counter was always zero on these maps because brush entities
  without an explicit "origin" key have s.origin == (0,0,0); the calculation now uses
  the center of r.absmin/r.absmax instead. Additionally, the start/finish entity was not
  being stored in the checkpoint array due to a late number assignment, leaving a NULL
  slot that froze the counter. The final segment from the last checkpoint to the finish
  line now counts down correctly, and the counter is clamped to zero once the player
  has finished the race.
